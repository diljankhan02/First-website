Milky Way

The Milky Way is our galaxy, a huge group of stars, planets, and dust held by gravity. On clear nights, it looks like a faint white band across the sky. Our solar system is a tiny part of this giant galaxy, which has billions of stars and stretches so wide that light takes 100,000 years to cross it.

Stargazing

Stargazing is the simple joy of looking at stars in the night sky. We can see twinkling stars, planets, and even the Milky Way. It is a peaceful way to feel connected to the universe and admire its vast beauty. The more we learn, the more magical the night sky becomes.
